About us





Agri'futur® is an ambitious start-up company founded by young students French people whose ideal is to be closer to nature in a world became too industrial. Our simple and green technologies have also have been developed with a view to raising young people's awareness of the Advantages of a species that needs to be protected without being opposed to it technological projects of the present... and the future!











Our services include

With Agri'futur®, produce the fruit of your desire in complete respect for nature in your garden, on your balcony or on the roof of your home.










Agri'future at the service of families:
Agri'futur products, ecologically and educationally valuable, are ideal for families with children. In fact, our greenhouses will allow them to observe and understand the fragile nature that surrounds them (observing the evolution of plants and the development and actions of the ladybirds through the installation of the beehive), while at the same time making them aware of the ecological cause and the good that is eaten, issues that are at the centre of interest today.

Agri'future, for a greener world:
In order to be able to offer you products of rarely achieved quality, Agri'futur works together with its greatest ally: nature!
Thanks to its product range, which is designed to limit the carbon footprint of its greenhouses as much as possible, Agri'futur guarantees full respect for the fragile nature that surrounds us.
With Agri'futur, no more pollution and other OMGs, and "Hello" well-being, happiness and health!

When ecology rhymes with economy :
It is often thought that ecology is spending money...wrongly! In fact, over 1 year and by producing 10 m² of vegetable garden for only a part of the year, 10 m² of vegetable garden can save more than 100 Euro of fresh vegetables! Agri'futur's greenhouses do not all have such a large surface area, but they protect your protégés much more than an open-air vegetable garden, all year round. At the same time, they save maintenance time and make the investment in purchasing your greenhouse quickly profitable! Now that it's on Agrifutur, you won't lose a radish!
